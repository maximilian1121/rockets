## What is this?
This mod adds two recreations of real life rockets into Minecraft!
This includes:
- The Saturn V's F1 engine
- The SpaceX raptor 3 engine

## Additional notes
I have spent SO MANY HOURS perfecting the physics and the way the particles look... I have aimed at getting the best looking particles for them and have learned a lot about NeoForge and the way minecraft rendering works!

Please take the time to enjoy and report issues against the mod!
